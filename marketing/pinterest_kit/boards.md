# Pinterest board structure — 6 boards

Create these 6 boards on Pinterest. Copy the **name** and **description**
verbatim. Pinterest favors keyword-front-loaded board names because they are
the strongest single signal of "what this account is about".

Use a board cover image once you have a few pins in each board (pick the
prettiest one — the Pinterest-pin PNG you already have).

## Why 6 not 10

More boards spreads each pin too thin and trains Pinterest to think your
account is unfocused. 6 boards × 17 pins × ~2 boards per pin = ~34
board-placements, which is enough density per board (5–8 pins each) for
Pinterest to recognise the topic.

When you write the next 17 articles, we can add 2 more boards (e.g.
"Europe Travel" once we have 3–4 European destinations). Not before.

## Why each pin can go on 2 boards

Pinterest counts the same pin saved to multiple boards as one piece of
content — but it gets shown in 2 board-category searches. Double exposure
for zero extra work. The `pins.csv` lists a primary + optional secondary
board for each pin.
