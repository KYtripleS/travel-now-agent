# Gently Yonder — Welcome email sequence (MailerLite-ready)

Paste each email into a MailerLite automation (Trigger: "subscriber joins group →
Newsletter"). Sender name **Gently Yonder**, reply-to an address you actually read.

Tone rules (match the site): plain, useful, no hype, no "hey traveler!!". One idea
per email. Affiliate links only where genuinely useful, always with the FTC
disclosure at the foot.

Standard disclosure footer to append to every email:

> Gently Yonder sometimes uses affiliate links. If you book or buy through one, we may
> earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. It never changes what we recommend.
> You're getting this because you asked for our Pre-Flight Checklist. Unsubscribe anytime.

---

Here's the checklist you asked for — two pages, printable, fridge-worthy:

**→ Download the Pre-Flight Checklist (PDF):**
https://gentlyyonder.com/downloads/travel-now-preflight-checklist.pdf

in your carry-on, never checked.** It's a global rule, and a checked bag with a loose

That's it for now. In a couple of days I'll send the short list of prep guides worth

Go gently,
Casey
Gently Yonder

## Notes for setup

- **Deliverability:** authenticate the gentlyyonder.com sending domain in MailerLite
  (Settings → Domains → add DKIM/SPF records in Namecheap DNS). Do this before the
  list grows; a gmail sender hurts inbox placement.
- **Cadence after the welcome series:** one email a week (see weekly-001.md for the
  inaugural issue and the reusable format). Re-use the best sections of new articles —
  don't write the newsletter from scratch.
- **The reply ask in Email 3 is the highest-value line in the whole sequence.** Replies
  train inbox providers that you're wanted mail, and the answers tell you what to
  research next. Don't cut it.
