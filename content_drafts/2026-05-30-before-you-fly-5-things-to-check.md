# Before You Fly: 5 Things to Check

> **Format:** Short-form video (under 35 seconds). Broad audience — any traveller, any destination.
> Lead with the hook in the first 2 seconds. Keep each point punchy — one sentence max.
> CTA: https://kytriples.github.io/travel-now-agent/checklist-generator.html

Most travel stress on flight day is preventable. These are the five checks that catch the problems people discover too late — at the gate, at security, or in the air.

---

## The 5 checks

**1. Documents ready**

Boarding pass on your phone or printed. Passport accessible — not buried in your bag. For international travel, check that your passport is valid for the full trip duration (requirements vary by destination and airline — check the official rules for your specific route).

**2. Carry-on liquids sorted**

All liquids in containers of 100ml / 3.4oz or under. Everything in one clear, resealable bag. That bag at the top of your carry-on — not the bottom — so you can pull it out in seconds at security.

**3. Devices charged, power bank packed**

Phone, earbuds, and tablet charged the night before. Portable power bank in your carry-on (power banks are not permitted in checked luggage on most airlines — verify your airline's rules). Cable packed with it.

**4. Data and connectivity confirmed**

SIM card, eSIM, or data roaming active before you leave. Offline maps downloaded for your destination. Screenshots of hotel address and confirmation numbers saved locally.

**5. Essentials in your carry-on, not checked**

Medications, a change of clothes, travel documents, and anything irreplaceable should travel with you in the cabin. Checked luggage can be delayed — personal items cannot.
