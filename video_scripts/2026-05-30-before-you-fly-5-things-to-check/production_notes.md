## Voiceover Script *(platform-neutral, ~60 seconds)*

Hey there, fellow traveller! Flight day doesn't have to be a scramble. Here are five quick checks to help you avoid last-minute airport stress.
First, ensure your documents are ready. Have your boarding pass easily accessible and confirm your passport is valid for the entire duration of your international trip, checking destination-specific requirements.
Second, sort your carry-on liquids. All containers must be 100ml or less, packed in one clear, resealable bag. Keep this bag at the top of your carry-on for swift removal at security.
Third, charge your devices and pack your power bank. Fully charge your phone, earbuds, and tablet the night before. Remember, portable power banks should always be in your carry-on, not checked luggage.
Fourth, confirm your data and connectivity. Activate your international SIM or eSIM before you leave. Download offline maps and save screenshots of vital information like your hotel address and confirmation numbers.
Finally, keep essentials in your carry-on. Medications, a change of clothes, travel documents, and anything irreplaceable should always travel with you in the cabin. This helps if checked luggage faces delays.
These five simple checks can make a big difference for a calmer, more confident start to your journey. Safe travels!
