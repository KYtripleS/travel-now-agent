**Hook** *(first ~3 seconds)*

**Full Script** *(~55 seconds)*
Hey travellers! [PAUSE] Want to avoid airport day stress? [PAUSE] Let's do five quick checks to make your journey smoother.
Number one: Documents ready. [PAUSE] Boarding pass handy, passport accessible, and always check its validity for your full trip duration. [PAUSE] Don't get caught out!
Number two: Carry-on liquids sorted. [PAUSE] Remember the 100ml rule and one clear, resealable bag. [PAUSE] Keep this bag right at the top of your carry-on for speed.
Number three: Devices charged and power bank packed. [PAUSE] Fully charge all your electronics. [PAUSE] Your portable power bank *must* be in your carry-on, not checked luggage.
Number four: Data and connectivity confirmed. [PAUSE] Get your international SIM or eSIM active early. [PAUSE] Download offline maps and save key info like hotel addresses as screenshots.
And number five: Essentials in your carry-on. [PAUSE] Medications, a change of clothes, travel documents, anything irreplaceable. [PAUSE] Always keep these with you, just in case checked bags are delayed.
Five simple checks for a stress-free departure! [PAUSE] Safe travels!
