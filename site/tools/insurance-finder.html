How the recommendation works

The finder maps your trip shape to the policy style that usually fits: a rolling subscription for open-ended and nomad travel, a fixed-term adventure policy for activity-heavy trips, and a simple single-trip policy for short getaways. Whatever it suggests, the deciding detail is always the exclusions — pre-existing conditions and your specific activities.
