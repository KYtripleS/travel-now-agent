Carry-on Prep

Airport security is easier when your liquids are ready before you leave home. This checklist helps you keep your carry-on liquids organized, easy to reach, and easier to inspect.

Before you fly with carry-on liquids

  1. Use small travel-size containers.
  2. Keep them in one clear bag.
  3. Pack the bag near the top of your carry-on.
  4. Check your airport, airline, or local security rules before leaving.

Why this helps

Liquids are one of the easiest things to forget until you reach security. Preparing them in advance may reduce last-minute repacking and make the screening process feel smoother.

Quick reminder

Liquid rules can vary by country, airport, and route. Always check the latest official guidance from your airport, airline, or transport security authority before your trip.

Travel Now tip: Keep your liquids bag near the top of your carry-on so you do not have to unpack everything at security.
