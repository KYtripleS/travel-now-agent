What happens to your answers

Reports are anonymous — no name, no email, no account. We aggregate them and show only statistics backed by at least five reports (“87% of travelers to Japan recommend carrying cash”), so one voice never masquerades as consensus. Free-text tips are read by our editors before anything is published. Every answer feeds the tools you already use here: the Ready Score, the destination map, and the guides themselves.
